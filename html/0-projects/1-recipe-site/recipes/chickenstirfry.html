Ingredients

  • Chicken (breast or thigh meat)
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Grain of choice (brown rice, quinoa, etc.)
  • Plenty of seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, chili powder, etc.)

Steps

  1. Put your grain of choice in a rice cooker with the appropriate water ratio, and set to cook.
  2. Wash and chop veggies.
  3. Add onions first, then bell peppers after a few minutes of cooking.
  4. While veggies cook, cube chicken and toss in bowl with cooking oil and seasoning. Careful with oil, lots of calories, low flavor.
  5. Once veggies are cooked, set aside in a bowl.
  6. Add chicken to a pan covered with cooking oil.
  7. After a few minutes, flip chicken or stir.
  8. Check doneness by grabbing the thickest couple cubes and cutting open with a knife to check for pinkness.
  9. Pair with cooked grains and enjoy!
