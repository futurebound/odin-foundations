Ingredients

  • Grass-Fed Ground Beef (1lb)
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Grain of choice (brown rice, quinoa, etc.)
  • Plenty of seasoning (salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, chili powder, etc.)

Steps

  1. Put your grain of choice in a rice cooker with the appropriate water ratio, and set to cook.
  2. Wash and chop veggies.
  3. Add grass fed butter to pan to cover, then add beef.
  4. While browning beef, add seasoning to taste. Don't be shy! Spices are low on calories and make bland food beautiful.
  5. Once browned, set aside beef, leaving liquid oils in pan.
  6. Add onions first, then bell peppers after a few minutes of cooking.
  7. Once onions are visibly soft, add beef back and mix together.
  8. Pair with cooked grains and enjoy!
