Ingredients

  • Oats (1/4 cup)
  • Hemp Hearts (3 tbsp)
  • Chia Seeds (3 tbsp)
  • Protein Powder (1 serving, optional)
  • Banana (or other fruit)
Nut Milk [optional]
  • Cashews (or base of choice for milk)
  • Dates
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put oats, hemp hearts, chia seeds, and protein powder in large mason jar
  2. Add own milk base (pre-made, or put all nut milk options in blender and blend til smooth)
  3. Stir mixture once, let sit for a few minutes. Then stir again (to prevent chia seeds from clumping)
